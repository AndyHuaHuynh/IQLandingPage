ONE PIECE
Monkey D. Luffy, a young man made of rubber, who, inspired by his childhood idol, the powerful pirate Red-Haired Shanks, sets off on a journey from the East Blue Sea to assemble a crew, find the mythical treasure, the One Piece, and proclaim himself the King of the Pirates!
